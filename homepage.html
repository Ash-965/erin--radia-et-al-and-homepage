You would be forgiven for thinking that everything we take for granted in the world of computer science and technology was created by men.Yet away from the public eye there have been women inventing, innovating and and pioneering the methods that have shaped how we use the internet today. Over the decades these women have dedicated their lives to the advancement of computer technology, yet to many of us, they have been as little more visible than ghosts. This website is dedicated to some of those women.
